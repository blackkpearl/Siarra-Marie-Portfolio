About Me

Hey there ~ my name is Siarra Guillory! I am a budding MERN full stack developer enrolled in a coding bootcamp through the University of Central Florida. I hold a B.A in Advertising and Mass Communications with a minor in Graphic Design. I have always had an interest in technology and application development and decided to take a leap of faith after some encouragement from my clan. During my academic career, I focused on my creativity, but later learned that creativity could also be applied in technology. I have 10 years of experience in advertising and graphic design. This new venture in web development evoked a passion that I felt in the creative realm. During this course, I have learned to never assume and to always trust in the process. I find myself enjoying the challenges and the creativity that is needed to solve complex problems. I am excited to see what the future brings and how I continue to grow as a junior web developer.


In my free time, I love running, anime, videogames and all vegan foods. As a creative, you can also find me creating new applications, sketching or traveling. As a web developer, I intend to chronicle all of my adventures and any new developments.
